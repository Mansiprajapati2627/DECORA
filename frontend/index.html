Frequently Asked Questions

What is Decora?

Decora is a professional online event decoration and planning service offering high-quality balloon decorations, birthday setups, anniversary décor, and more across multiple cities in India.

How can I book a decoration service?

You can book directly through our website by selecting your package and theme, then confirming your booking details. It takes just a few minutes!

Do you offer decoration services at home?

Yes, we provide decoration services at homes, banquet halls, corporate venues, and more.

Which cities do you provide services in?

We are currently available in major cities across India. Please check our contact section for more details on service areas.

Do I need to provide any materials?

No, we bring all the necessary decoration materials with us. You just need to provide the venue details.

How do I contact Decora for support?

You can reach us via our contact section below, call our helpline, or use WhatsApp chat for quick assistance.
